About Me

My journey began in the precise world of medical engineering, where I spent years ensuring that life-saving equipment operated flawlessly in healthcare facilities across Kenya. This technical foundation taught me the critical importance of attention to detail and systematic problem-solving.

The transition to virtual assistance and customer support wasn't just a career change—it was a natural evolution of my passion for helping people. Whether I was calibrating medical devices or resolving customer inquiries, the common thread has always been my commitment to improving people's experiences and outcomes.

My core values—clarity, service, and adaptability—guide everything I do. I believe in transparent communication, going above and beyond for those I serve, and continuously evolving to meet new challenges in our rapidly changing digital landscape.
